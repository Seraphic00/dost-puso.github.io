About Us

The DOST-PSU UCC Scholarsâ€™ Organization, affectionately known as DOST-PUSO, had its humble beginnings on October 1, 2022. Founded by a group of passionate Department of Science and Technology (DOST) scholars at Pangasinan State University's Urdaneta City Campus, the organization's inception marked the commencement of a remarkable journey.

As DOST-PUSO continues to grow, adapt, and evolve, it remains steadfast in its mission to inspire and empower scholars, promote scientific literacy, and serve the nation with unwavering dedication. The organization's journey is a testament to what can be achieved when passionate individuals come together with a shared vision, driven by a love for education, science, and service to the Philippines.

In a short span of time, DOST-PUSO achieved remarkable milestones. The organization became a hub for fostering academic excellence, nurturing leadership skills, and promoting patriotic values. It organized numerous training workshops, seminars, and outreach programs that not only enhanced the skills and knowledge of its members but also extended the benefits of science and technology to the wider community.
